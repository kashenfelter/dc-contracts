        On July 31, 2013, the parties filed a Consent Motion to Stay Proceedings, reciting that the
parties have tentatively settled the instant appeal. Accordingly, D-1125 is stayed until August 28,
2013, pending the parties’ finalizing a settlement agreement and the filing of Appellant’s stipulation
of dismissal with prejudice. The August 5, 2013, hearing on the merits is continued cancelled. The
parties are requested to file a joint status update no later than August 28, 2013, if a stipulation of
dismissal with prejudice has not been filed by that date.

SO ORDERED.
