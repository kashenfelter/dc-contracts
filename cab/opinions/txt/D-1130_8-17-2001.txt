AMENDMENT TO ORDER ON APPELLEE’S MOTION FOR STAY OF PENDING APPEAL

        By order dated March 28, 2001, in order to insure an orderly presentation of this matter
before the Board, the Board stayed all action in this proceeding. The Board has been informed by
counsel for the District “that the parties continue their settlement discussions” and jointly request
that the stay be continued.

       Based on the representation of counsel, the Board amends the order of March 28, 2001 to
extend the stay in this matter until September 17, 2001.


       SO ORDERED.
