                                           ORDER

               By letter dated August 18, 1992, and received by the Board on
          September 3, 1992, the Leary School requested to withdraw its
          appeal under Contract No. C10030.   At the October 21, 1992, pre-
          hearing conference in the above-captioned consolidated appeals,
          the District indicated that it does not oppose the withdrawal.

               Wherefore, finding that Appellant wishes to withdraw its
          appeal and that there is no opposition thereto, the Board,
          pursuant to Board Rule 121, 36 DCMR 2697-2698 dismisses the
          appeal, without prejudice.

                  So ORDERED.
