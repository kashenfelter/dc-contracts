                                    ORDER DISMISSING PROTEST

        On November 13, 2012, World Energy Solutions, Inc. (“protester”) filed the instant protest as an
unsuccessful bidder to the above-referenced Solicitation. Following its review of the Agency Report, on
December 12, 2012, protester confirmed that it did not object to the District’s request that the Board
dismiss this protest. Accordingly, we dismiss CAB No. P-0929 with prejudice.
